Interactive web · Creative systems

Tarocchi

An interactive narrative web experience with 24 branching story paths, replayable state-driven progression, parallax, and synchronized audio.

PATHS24
EFFECTSPARALLAX
AUDIOSYNCHRONIZED

Role

Front-end engineer and interaction designer

Challenge

What had to be solved

Twenty-four story branches had to feel cinematic without allowing animation, audio, and route state to drift apart.

Approach

How I built it

  1. Represented narrative progress as explicit state instead of coupling it to visual components.
  2. Synchronized scene transitions and audio cues from the same route events.
  3. Used reusable parallax and motion primitives to keep visual behavior consistent.

Outcomes

What changed

  • 24 replayable paths
  • Reusable scene pipeline
  • Synchronized motion and audio
